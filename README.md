# Blade Storm
## About
Blade storm is a minecraft mod made for version 1.21.1 (fabric). It is based off of an agent in valorant named "Jett".
I tried to mimic her abilities in this mod.

## Features
- Dash: You can press Q (default) to dash towards a small distance with a cooldown of four seconds.
- UpDraft: You can press Alt Key(default) to launch yourself up in the air, again with a cooldown of four seconds.
- Ultimate Ability - BladeStorm: Jett's Ultimate ability is the real deal with a cooldown of 10 seconds, you can press X to get 5 Throwing Knives which damages the entity upon contact(3 Hearts).
- Drift: You can hold Space after jumping or falling from ledge to slowly fall towards the ground, there is no fall damage upon contact with the ground.

## Setup
1. Download blade-storm-1.0.0.jar from the releases.
2. Drag the jar file into your mods folder (make sure you're on version 1.21.1).
